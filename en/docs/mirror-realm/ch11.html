Chapter 11: Victor's Calculations

Taiwan Edition v2​

Location: Mirror Realm · Emerald Isle Time: April—August 1024 Protagonist: Lin Zhao-ming

His arrival was not preceded by a formal introduction.

It manifested as an email packet. Subject field: Organizational Update — New Leadership Alignment.

Lin Zhao-ming initiated the read sequence at his hot-desk. Deployed by Peter. The overall packet execution was brutally brief. "To optimize alignment with macroscopic strategic vectors, we are extremely pleased to announce the integration of Mr. Victor into the Emerald Isle command tier, assuming the designation of Operational Efficiency Consultant. Victor deploys with an extensive archive of cross-global operational telemetry, having executed tours across numerous premier corporate entities."

The exact identities of those entities were not defined. Lin Zhao-ming would later acquire the intel: Ling-Guang Dynamics, General Prism, Nordic Signal. The identical swarm of consultants. Perpetually rotating across the grid. The terminal output is always derived from the exact same mathematical textbook.

The terminal string of the email: "Victor will initiate localized one-on-one syncs with all functional units starting next Monday to parse current atmospheric conditions. Anticipating absolute compliance from the network."

Lin Zhao-ming parsed the string "parse current atmospheric conditions." Having executed within this architecture for three solar cycles, he possessed the requisite cryptographic keys to translate it. "Parse current atmospheric conditions" mathematically equaled: You transmit, he intercepts, and subsequently, he executes the exact identical algorithm he had already hardcoded prior to breaching the room.

But what caused a catastrophic miscalculation on his end was—Victor was a completely disparate classification of entity.

The preceding operators—Peter, Vincent, the Boss—every kinetic output they generated possessed a universal biometric signature: They mandated that you believed in the projection. They mandated you validated their generated reality as absolute truth. They mandated your assimilation of their version of the architecture. Therefore, they persistently engaged in active narrative generation. Active justification. Deployment of rhetorical algorithms. Using syntax strings like "Don't fixate on localized metrics" or "Acknowledge the subterranean sacrifices" to cosmetically wrap the raw data.

Victor possessed zero requirement for any of that.

Monday. A fresh node spawned on the calendar matrix. Thirty-minute block. "1:1 — Victor / Zhao-Ming".

Lin Zhao-ming breached the perimeter. Extremely compact briefing room. A singular circular interface. Victor was already anchored to a coordinate.

Initial optical profile: Absolute acoustic zero.

It wasn't a synthetically generated, hostile silence. It wasn't the hollow, vacuous silence Peter projected. It was the silence of a biological unit executing what he calculated to be the absolute most efficient algorithm—passively ingesting your transmission—so he consequently generated zero unnecessary kinetic friction. A physical laptop deployed on the surface. A writing stylus. A physical notebook matrix. Geometrically pristine, as if freshly spawned from the manufacturer.

"Zhao-Ming." He utilized the exact identifier. Flawless phonetic execution. Not the brutally mangled Latinized translation. The kinetic output of an operator who had actively queried the database for the correct auditory protocol prior to engagement. "Thank you for making time."

Lin Zhao-ming assumed a coordinate.

"I intend to map the architecture of your active operations. Your designated function. The specific friction bottlenecks you've isolated via your optical sensors."

The auditory frequency was perfectly flatlined. Devoid of a smile. Yet equivalently devoid of hostility. Functionally identical to a sheet of pristine ballistic glass. You project your optics into it, assuming you are penetrating to the far side, but the reality is you are merely observing your own reflection.

Lin Zhao-ming initiated transmission.

He dumped the telemetry of his active operations. Dumped the infinite loops the external supplier was trapped within. Transmitted the exact geographic coordinates of the null pointer (an architectural logic error where the system attempts to fetch data from an empty memory block, typically detonating a catastrophic system crash). Transmitted the absolute decoupling existing between the core design architecture and the supply chain pipeline. Following the execution of each localized packet, Victor physically logged a microscopic string of syntax into his notebook. Minimal data. Incredibly lethally precise strokes.

Approaching the core design error vector, Lin Zhao-ming actively throttled his transmission velocity. He aggressively audited his syntax selection. Because within this specific corporate ecosystem, the string "design harbors an error" possessed massive explosive yield. He had experimented with deploying it previously. He had deployed it directly into the HR mainframe. He possessed the exact telemetry of the fallout.

"The initialization sequence within the UIP Firmware (the subterranean control logic bridging the bare-metal hardware and the macroscopic system) harbors a lethal null pointer anomaly. The root catastrophe resides natively within the system integration strata. It absolutely does not originate from the supplier's coordinate."

Victor inputted data. Ceased kinetic motion. Raised optics.

"Do you possess the raw telemetry?"

"Affirmative. I generated an exhaustive analytical matrix. Partial logs reside within the ALC architecture. However, the comprehensive backend logs—I lack the cryptographic clearance to access them."

Victor maintained optical lock for three seconds. Potentially four seconds.

"ALC maintains a physical log. You should logically possess a functional vector to extract the required data."

"Regarding the backend sector specifically, my access is firewall-blocked."

Victor maintained optical lock.

"Then calculate a bypass protocol."

Silence.

Not a silence saturated with localized friction. Not a silence engineered to apply targeted atmospheric pressure. It was an absolutely translucent, mechanically pure silence—Victor mathematically did not classify this as an anomaly. "Your access is firewall-blocked" was an equation he possessed zero mandate to solve. It was a localized error strictly assigned to your queue.

Lin Zhao-ming maintained his coordinate. He had deployed this exact packet to Peter previously. To the Boss. To HR. The Boss would deploy, "Don't fixate on localized metrics." HR would successfully mistranslate his transmission into, "Specific technical anomalies, currently tracked."

Followed by an instant progression.

"What do you calculate is the primary macro-bottleneck currently stalling the hive's velocity?"

Lin Zhao-ming maintained visual lock on the entity. It wasn't that Victor had dropped the packet containing the preceding transmission. He had intercepted it flawlessly. He had logged it. And subsequently, he had merely designated that specific anomaly to a solitary coordinate on a specific line of his notebook, perfectly aligned alongside every other extraneous variable. Design error. Null pointer. Supplier. They all consumed the exact identical geometric volume of data on Victor's notebook. Not a single variable possessed an augmented explosive yield.

Because within Victor's processing matrix, every single event natively classified simply as input. Not an anomaly. Merely telemetry.

"Copy that. Terminal parameter. I aggressively mandate you inject significantly more velocity into these projects. The current timeline vectors—are clear for severe compression."

"The latency affecting the timeline vectors is absolutely not originating from my localized coordinate—"

"I possess that context. Nevertheless, terminal yield must manifest prior to the Q3 hard-stop. Run your algorithms and execute."

Victor broke the sitting posture to stand. Extended a physical manipulator. Initiated a handshake. The biological surface was devoid of moisture. The kinetic pressure was mathematically perfect.

"Thank you, Zhao-Ming. Very helpful."

The briefing room physical barrier sealed.

Lin Zhao-ming idled at his coordinate for a prolonged temporal segment prior to extracting.

His cognitive processors were not locked onto the semantic strings Victor had spoken. They were processing an atmospheric sensation he possessed zero historical logs of experiencing.

Every single preceding operator—Peter, Vincent, Cindy, the Boss—the operations they executed, regardless of how catastrophically deranged, all shared a singular foundational architecture: They mandated Lin Zhao-ming's existence within their reality matrix. He functioned as a node requiring active management. An acoustic signal requiring active mistranslation. An anomaly requiring active processing. Nevertheless, his existence was acknowledged.

Victor bypassed that entire architecture.

On Victor's physical notebook, Lin Zhao-ming's identifier might momentarily occupy a specific line. But that line was mathematically indistinguishable from any other line within the matrix. He wasn't registered as a biological operator. He was registered as a batch of inputs. His raw analytics were classified as noise data. His localized frustration was classified as atmospheric static. Victor would algorithmically harvest the optimized outputs and instantly vaporize the redundant static. Not maliciously driven. Purely because within his operating system, the taxonomic category for "Biological Human" simply didn't exist. The only active parameters were "Optimized" and "Redundant."

Lin Zhao-ming extracted from the briefing room. The corridor acoustics were completely flat. A distant node was executing a printing sequence. The rhythmic mechanical strike of the hardware cutting through the silence.

For the very first chronological instance, he generated a realization: The preceding swarm of operators—the exact nodes who actively invalidated him, mistranslated him, and geometrically distorted him—at minimum, they possessed the sensors to detect his presence in the blast radius.

Victor hadn't even executed that baseline handshake protocol.

Over the subsequent operational weeks, Victor's existence manifested akin to ambient room temperature. Not an instantaneous thermal collapse. A sensation where you suddenly process that you lack the logs detailing exactly when your chassis began vibrating violently from the freeze.

The density of scheduled syncs violently spiked. However, the geometric structure of the syncs mutated.

Every single slide initiated with one or two abbreviated strings establishing macro-context. Lethally brief. Devastatingly clear. Target product identifier. Percentage of total global revenue affected. Raw monetary scale of the blast radius. Followed instantaneously by—an unfiltered dump of pure numeric data.

One or two strings of context.

What was the absolute maximum contextual clarity you could achieve? At best, you established the macro-identifier of the event. At best, you established the topological scale of the damage. But every single granular variable trapped in the void between "the event" and "why the event spawned"—the entirety of the subterranean technical architecture, the entirety of the kinetic operational struggles, three solar cycles of historical legacy, the brutal grinding output of hundreds of biological nodes—all of that mass was violently compressed into those one or two localized strings. And then permanently evaporated.

But if you possessed Lin Zhao-ming's localized database—if you possessed the cryptographic keys decoding the horrors buried beneath those integers—you observed an entirely disparate reality: The ultimate function of this geometric formatting was absolutely not to decrypt the anomaly. It was to execute a legally sanctioned vaporization of the anomaly's true complexity.

A fifteen-minute duration. A singular project review loop. A solitary node transmitting two or three solar cycles of grinding combat within a fifteen-minute uplink. No computational engine in existence could successfully untangle that density. Every variable was aggregated into generalized numerics—meticulously engineered generalizations. Yet, regardless of the granular precision applied to the generalization, it fundamentally remained a generalization.

Personnel bandwidth? Personnel bandwidth was merely a tertiary variable manipulated to modify the primary three. Increase bandwidth = increase fiscal bleed, but yields accelerated temporal velocity. Decrease bandwidth = fiscal optimization, but extends the temporal axis. As for the numerics—yield parameters (the percentage of functional product passing QC versus the total manufactured volume), defect rates—whether they could be successfully mutated depended entirely on the immediate tactical configuration.

Halted optical sweep on a specific slide. The yield curve for Supplier A exhibited a severe geometric collapse.

"Right here," Victor pinned the specific coordinate. "What is the localized root cause of this system failure?"

Lin Zhao-ming possessed the decryption key. The catastrophic failure originated from the UIP Firmware's null pointer. But transmitting that specific payload within this confined perimeter would instantly trigger a rejection algorithm.

An exogenous node transmitted: "The supplier's manufacturing process controls experienced stochastic volatility. We have aggressively initiated tracking protocols."

Victor executed a mechanical nod. "What is the projected temporal timeline for resolution?"

"A two-week buffer."

"Compress the timeline to a singular seven-day cycle."

"Copy that."

Execution terminated. Just like that.

Victor didn't query, "What is the fundamental architectural root cause generating the anomaly?" Not because he lacked the semantic capability to phrase the query. Because the concept "root cause" simply did not natively compile within his executable logic. Within his operational equation, the only valid integer arrays were: Yield, Timeline, Cost. Degraded yield → Suppler mandated to execute optimization. Timeline latency exceeds thresholds → Compress timeframe. Cost exceeds projections → Execute fiscal cuts.

Every single binary decision possessed flawless computational logic. Every single geometric step could be legally defended. Every single meeting log would survive a lethal forensic audit.

But not a single geometric step would organically intersect with the actual root cause. Because the root cause resided at a geographically elevated strata. And Victor's equation—identical to the equation driving every single biological unit infesting this corporation—was hardcoded to exclusively run downward sweeps.

Lin Zhao-ming held his coordinate in the sync, monitoring Victor legally attribute the catastrophic yield collapse to "manufacturing process control volatility." The impulse to transmit an override roared in his processors. But he possessed perfect system logs detailing the exact terminal output of his preceding override attempts.

He maintained absolute acoustic zero.

Yet there remained one specific topological anomaly Lin Zhao-ming’s CPU failed to compile.

He had actively maintained surveillance on the matrix for three solar cycles. Every single iteration.

Every single quarter.

Bypassing the psychotic cycle of endlessly waiting until the DVT cliff-edge to frantically scramble for a workaround.

Organizational geometry. Human resources allocations. Political leverage. Fiscal liquidity.
