## About This Book

**GameVictory** is a work of technology history and analysis, utilizing a "five-act detective narrative structure" throughout the book,  
tracing how the video game industry inadvertently laid the foundation for the AI revolution, the smartphone era, the global semiconductor order, and the geopolitical landscape over the past forty years.

The core thesis of this book: **Gamers are the most powerful unofficial R&D funding pool in human history.**

## About the Author

**星忘塵 · Nebula Walker**

A computer kid who grew up in Mong Kok's Sino Centre. Graduated with a double major in EEE and Computer Engineering.  
Tweaked `config.sys` under DOS, built game consoles on HC11 microcontrollers,  
and read Valve's Proton source code under Linux.  
A writer with an engineer's perspective spanning both hardware and software—from transistors to the cloud.
